How to play
1
Add the bot to a group and message it in DM at least once so it can send you role cards.
2
Type /mafia in the group to open a lobby. Min. 4 players.
3
Everyone presses ✋ Join game. The creator presses 🚀 Start.
4
🌙 Night: Special roles get DMs with action buttons. You have 45 seconds to act.
5
☀️ Day: Everyone discusses for 60 seconds, then votes who to eliminate.
6
Repeat until one team wins!
Win conditions
🕊️
Civilians win
All mafia members are eliminated by vote or detective.
🔫
Mafia wins
Mafia count ≥ civilian count (they control the vote).
Role distribution
4 players → 1 Mafia, 1 civilian, + specials
5 players → 1 Mafia + Doctor added
6+ players → Detective added
Every 3 players = +1 Mafia
🔫 Mafia tip: Don't vote unanimously in the same direction. Split your votes to look innocent.
🩺 Doctor tip: In early rounds, heal the most vocal players — they're likely targeted by mafia.
🔍 Detective tip: Don't reveal your role until you have solid proof. The mafia will target you immediately.
🧑 Civilian tip: Watch who defends who. Mafia members rarely vote against each other.
🗳 Voting tip: If someone pushes hard to eliminate a player without much evidence, be suspicious.
😶 Silence tip: Very quiet players are suspicious. Mafia often stays low to avoid attention.
📊 Math tip: With 4 players alive and 1 mafia, one wrong vote = game over for civilians.
🎭 Bluffing tip: If you're mafia and suspected, claim to be the Doctor — it's harder to disprove.
